Dink & Rally Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Dink & Rally is an iPhone app for organizing pickleball round robins. The app is designed to keep your tournament information on your iPhone.

Information The App Stores

Dink & Rally can store information you enter into the app, including event names, player names, team pairings, matches, scores, standings, event templates, and app settings.

This information is stored locally on your iPhone. Local storage means the information is saved on your device, not on a Dink & Rally server.

Contacts Import

Dink & Rally may ask for permission to access Contacts only when you choose to add players from your contacts.

  • The app uses Contacts to help you add player names without typing them manually.
  • The app saves only the contact names you select as player names.
  • The app does not save phone numbers, email addresses, home addresses, or your full address book.
  • The app does not scan your contacts in the background.

Accounts, Ads, Tracking, And Selling Data

  • Dink & Rally does not require an account.
  • Dink & Rally does not show ads.
  • Dink & Rally does not sell your personal information.
  • Dink & Rally does not upload your event data to a Dink & Rally server.

Exporting And Sharing

If you export or share event information, your iPhone may show Apple's share sheet so you can choose where to send the file or information.

After you choose another app or service to receive the information, that app or service is responsible for how it handles the information.

Deleting Your Information

You can delete event information inside the app by deleting events. You can also remove the app and its local data from your iPhone by deleting the Dink & Rally app.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated when the app changes. The updated date at the top of this page will show when the policy was last changed.

Support

For support, use the support contact listed on the Dink & Rally App Store page.
